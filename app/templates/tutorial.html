Here's the tutorial:

1. Download the material from this LINK
2. Click "Choose File" and select the image you want to crop. Then, click "Open".

3. Adjust the dimensions of the image cropping by adjusting the cropping box or entering the dimensions manually.
4. Once satisfied with the selection, press "CROP" to cut the image according to the specified dimensions.

5. The detection result will be displayed, allowing you to compare the cropped result with the original image.

6. To restart the detection process, press "RESET" to remove detection artifacts and restore the application to its initial state.

About

Elliptocytes, ovalocytes, teardrop cells, and stomatocytes are morphological variations in red blood cells, each displaying unique shapes such as oval, teardrop, or mouth-like. These irregular shapes often signify potential health issues related to hematopoiesis or red blood cell membrane integrity.
